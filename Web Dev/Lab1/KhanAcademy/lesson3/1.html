Links for learning

  • Khan Academy: A platform offering free online courses and lessons in various subjects, including math, science, economics, history, and more.

  • Coursera: A platform that provides access to online courses from universities and organizations worldwide. Courses cover a wide range of topics, from computer science to humanities.

  • TED-Ed: TED-Ed provides lessons worth sharing, covering a diverse range of topics in the form of animated videos. It's a great platform for learning through engaging content.
